Video Still Life

What is a Still Life?

A still life is an exersize commonly used by most artists to practice drawing or painting objects and people from life. Still lives typically consist of artsits stood and sat around a subject, which could be either objects, or people.

The Assignment

The assignment we were given was to set up a still life, specifically using a moving object and a reflective object, and record and edit together two 30 second long videos. After recieving this prompt, I chose to centre the still life around my turntable and record set up, naming it "Record Decor".

The still life consisted of:

    - Record Player Whilst Moving
    - A Record On Top
    - Lego Cactus
    - A Figurine On The Record
    - The Record Cleaning Brush and Cleaning Solution
